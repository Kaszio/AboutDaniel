Daniel Keller
Flux CEO/Co-Founder

Daniel Keller is a prominent figure within the Flux project, serving as the Chief Strategy Officer (CSO) and one of the project's core team members. With a background in information technology and extensive experience in the blockchain industry, he has played a crucial role in the development and growth of the Flux ecosystem.

Keller is known for his passion for decentralization and his commitment to building scalable, secure, and user-friendly blockchain solutions. As the CSO of Flux, his responsibilities include formulating the project's strategic vision, ensuring its long-term success, and fostering partnerships within the industry.

Under his leadership, the Flux project has developed and launched numerous innovative solutions, such as the Proof of Useful Work (PoUW) protocol, which has garnered significant attention in the blockchain and gaming communities. Keller's expertise and dedication have been integral to Flux's rapid growth and ongoing success in the world of decentralized technology.

Flux is a decentralized Web3 cloud infrastructure comprised of user-operated, scalable and globally distributed computational nodes.
